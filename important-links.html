Our Trips Categories

Tour du Mont Blanc

The Tour du Mont Blanc is a long distance trekking trail passing through France, Switzerland and Italy. The complete circuit is approximately 107 miles (170 km) and has an altitude gain and loss of over 6.2 miles (10 km), which is more than the height of Everest.

Patagonia

Patagonia is an adventurer's paradise; impressive granite towers form a skyline of jagged peaks, milky-blue water roars below in rivers that carry lonely icebergs fed from imposing glaciers.

From Scratch

A From Scratch journey offers all of the benefits of an organized tour, with the flexibility and exclusivity of indepedent travel. You choose your own dates and we help organize the itinerary, locations, guides, and exclusive experiences.

Eco lodges & Tours

Stay in lodging that puts nature, conservation and environmental protection at the forefront of their offering. Focus on sustainable tourism.

Our Service Categories

Consult & Book

We help you understand product offerings from our carefully selected supplier base. We book lodging and scheduled group tours as well as related transfers and logistics where necessary

Pygmy Itineraries

We create unique itineraries by combining lodging, transportation, equipment and experiences and handling complex logistics. These itineraries can be self guided or privately guided and can be tailored to suit your desires. This service will provide you with a personalized itinerary, guide / guidebooks, maps and all info to get you from A to Z for you to enjoy everything in between.

From Scratch

You bring us an idea and we turn it into reality. We draw your exclusive journey from scratch including complex logistics, experiences, guides and/or guidebooks, lodging, equipment, and itinerary. Tell us your epic idea, and will make it happen.

Talk to our Adventure Expert

Pygmy Elephant is how he spreads his love for adventure and self discovery in the world.
